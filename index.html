DeMoya Music

Music

Born and raised in The Bronx, New York, DeMoya has found success through music as a writer. She contributed to the 2021 Grammy nominated album, It All Comes Back Love by her close friend and professional mentor Maxi Priest, produced by Shaggy, Shane Hoosong and Dwayne Shippy. As well as her success as a songwriter, DeMoya has always been a performer and has sung professionally on and off throughout her life.

It was following a chance meeting with legendary singer Maxi Priest backstage at his concert that DeMoya and Maxi formed a strong collaborative relationship. DeMoya was introduced to producer Livingstone Brown and together the three of them set to work creating a brand new project that put DeMoya front and centre.
